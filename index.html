All About Iguanas


What are Iguanas?

Iguanas are a group of lizards with an omnivourous diet, and scaly skin.
There are many types of iguanas. One of the most famous is the green iguana.
Iguanas can grow up to 6 feet long, and have a third eye on their head that detects brightness.
Iguanas have sharp eyesight that can see things from far away.

Iguanas as pets?
Iguanas are growing largely in terms of popularity as household pets
Although demanding to take care of, they are beautiful animals to observe.
Iguanas require specific temperatures and light in order to thrive.
Iguanas can show affection, if loved and cared for throughout its life.

Iguanas as food?
Iguana meat is a staple in some regions of Mexico, as well as the United States.
As an invasive species in the United States, they are being taken care of by consumption.
Green iguanas are the prefered type of iguana to consume, due to its tenderness.
Iguana can be fried, boiled, or grilled, and with good seasoning, can be delicious.
